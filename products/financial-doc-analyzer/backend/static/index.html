Questions

Is this financial advice?

No. ClariFi produces an automated draft to help you understand a document faster. It's a starting point for your own review — always verify figures against the source and consult a professional for decisions.

Do you store my document?

No. Your file is analyzed in memory and not saved. We only keep anonymous usage counts so we can improve the product.

What documents work best?

Annual reports, 10-Ks, earnings releases, financial statements, and bank or fund statements. Both normal and scanned image-only PDFs work — scans are read with OCR.

How accurate is it?

The AI is instructed never to invent figures and to flag anything uncertain, especially on scanned documents. It's accurate enough to orient you quickly, but it's a draft — double-check anything you'll act on.

Is it free?

Right now, yes — analyze documents at no cost while we're in early access. Join the waitlist to hear when paid features like batch analysis and saved reports arrive.
