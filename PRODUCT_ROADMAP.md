## Phase 3 Product Details

### 5. Tender & RFP Response AI
Government and corporate tenders require weeks of work to write a compelling response. Most companies lose because the writing is weak, not because their offer is bad. AI reads the tender document and drafts a professional, compliant response. High-ticket B2B transaction — no subscription required to start making money.

**Why it's fast:** Rwanda, Kenya, and Ghana governments issue thousands of tenders monthly. Five paying clients in Kigali = USD 5,000–10,000/month immediately. Shared doc extraction infrastructure — minimal new build.

---

### 6. AI Contract & Legal Document Review
Every SME, freelancer, and startup signs contracts they don't fully understand. AI highlights risks, extracts key obligations and deadlines, and explains clauses in plain language. Lawyers charge USD 300/hour — this product pays for itself in the first use.

**Positioning:** "Understanding documents" not "giving legal advice" — no licence required anywhere. Global from day 1.

---

### 7. Grant Writing AI
NGOs collectively apply for billions in grants annually. Most are rejected because the applications are poorly written. AI reads the grant brief and writes a compelling, formatted application draft that the team then refines.

**Africa angle:** Rwanda alone has hundreds of active NGOs. One partnership with an NGO umbrella body = 50 paying clients instantly.

---

### 8. Commodities Intelligence for African Traders
Coffee, tea, cocoa, copper — commodity prices directly affect millions of livelihoods in East and West Africa. No clean mobile-first, AI-powered tool exists for small traders to understand price movements and what they mean for their business.

**RSE connection:** The existing pipeline already tracks prices and generates AI commentary. Swap equity tickers for commodity codes — most of the infrastructure is already built.

---

### 9. White-label AI Agent Platform
Other businesses want what RSE Intelligence has built: a team of AI agents running their operations autonomously. Sell the orchestrator as a managed service — branded with the client's team names, integrated into their workflows.

**Why last:** Longest enterprise sales cycle. But highest ACV (USD 6,000–24,000 ARR per client) and most defensible — clients build their operations around it and don't churn.
